WHAT IS DIGIMON?


Digimon (Japanese: デジモン, Hepburn: Dejimon, branded as
Digimon: Digital Monsters, stylized as DIGIMON), short for
"Digital Monsters" (デジタルモンスター Dejitaru Monsuta), is
a Japanese media franchise encompassing virtual pet toys,
anime, manga, video games, films and a trading card game.
The franchise focuses on the eponymous creatures,
who inhabit a "Digital World", a parallel universe that originated
from Earth's various communication networks.
